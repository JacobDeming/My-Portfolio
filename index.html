About Me

Ever since I was five years-old and received my first gaming console - an old monochrome Game Boy - I have been hooked on video games and their innate ability to immerse audiences in worlds separate from our own. Unlike my friends who simply enjoyed playing games as a means to unwind and pass the time, however, I found myself growing more and more interested in the actual study of video games and those techniques by which developers create digital playgrounds.

While the interactive nature of games and the puzzling complexity of their design were those elements that originally drew me into the field of Media Studies during college, I soon found myself following the path of an independent developer after graduation. Having joined up with Starboard Games LLC as their PR Lead, I now work alongside the gaming startup's many coders, artists, and writers as their liason to the grander gaming community. During my time with the company, I have developed a web-based publicity campaign while also increasing our social media presence online. As the project has matured, I have also begun organizing and directing our participation in video game conventions as a means to develop interest and prepare us for our eventual crowdfunding campaigns.

While the work is rewarding, I have found a desire to develop my own coding skills with the ultimate objective of joining in the design process fully. It is for this reason that I have entered into Rutgers' Coding Boot Camp and taken my first steps towards becoming a proficient coder, designer, and developer.
